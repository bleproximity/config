This is the official app for the Ericsson Open Space event in Linköping, 2015.<BR><BR>Ericsson Open Space 2015 gives the visitors an insight into the different activities at Ericsson R&D Center, Linköping. This app provides positioning within the area and proximity based information about the different activities. In addition, the app provides quiz questions to be answered at a separate quiz map, and letters to be rearranged to form a word.<BR><BR>
The map tab provides a crude positioninig when at the Open Space event. The notification tab lists three types of information:<BR>-Open Space station information<BR>-Quiz questions<BR>-Letters.<BR><BR>Quiz Instruction: questions will pop up as you walk by them. All QUIZ questions are found within the Open Space area! Type the correct answer to the questions on your printed quiz map and leave the answers in the RockShop when done!<BR><BR>Statistics from the serverside is presented at booth 5.<BR><BR> Found letters are gathered under a separate tab, where the letters can be moved around to form a word to be reported at booth 5.<BR><BR>The app has been developed by Benjamin Wiberg, and the server side by Erik Bertilsson, with support and input from Fredrik Gunnarsson, Mats Ahlbom and Daniel Jakobsson.
